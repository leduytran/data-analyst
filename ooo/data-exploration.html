# Visualization
def heatmap(title, df, width, height, snum=False, **settings):
    sns.set_style()
    fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(width,height))
    if snum: ax = sns.heatmap(df, cbar=0, linewidths=2, square=True, annot=True, fmt="d", **settings)
    else: ax = sns.heatmap(df, cbar=0, linewidths=2, square=True, **settings)
    plt.title(title)
    plt.show()
    
def barchart(title, df, width,height, xv, yv, **settings):
    sns.set_style()
    fig, ax = plt.subplots(figsize=(width,height))
    ax = sns.barplot(x=xv, y=yv, data=df, **settings)
    plt.title(title)
    plt.show()
    
    
# Pivot Table
def pivot_value(df, rows, columns, calc_field):
    df_pivot = pd.pivot_table(df,values=calc_field, 
                              index=rows, 
                              columns=columns, 
                              fill_value=0)
    return df_pivot

def pivot_count(df, rows, columns, calc_field):
    df_pivot = pd.pivot_table(df,values=calc_field, 
                              index=rows, 
                              columns=columns, 
                              aggfunc=np.size,
                              fill_value=0
                             ).dropna(axis=0, how='all')
    return df_pivot

def pivot_sum(df, rows, columns, calc_field):
    df_pivot = pd.pivot_table(df,values=calc_field, 
                              index=rows, 
                              columns=columns, 
                              aggfunc=np.sum,
                              fill_value=0
                             ).dropna(axis=0, how='all')
    return df_pivot

Interpretation¶

  • The percentages of parents who have more than 2 kids signed contract is 11.6% while the number of parents who have only one kid is 86.6%
  • Genernal communication is typically the most learning goal that the parents seeks for their kids, and the most English solution they found is Offline English center.
  • In the big city (Ho Chi Minh), digital channels like Facebook and Website have the most customers. Facebook is also the best channel to approach other cities (which are in small and medium range).
  • Referral is the number #2 channel that we should invest into.
  • August is the month with typically the most contracts from 2019 to 2020.
  • The #1 of contract value is 3,744,390,000 generally linked to children learning in the primary school years in the big city (HCM) and the second rank is 1,652,863,000 belonged to kids learning in the kindergarten in medium cities (Hanoi, Binh Duong, and Dong Nai provinces)
  • The best Seller in 2020 is Li Nguyn Hng n with 87 contracts that has its value 2,239,500,000. She also performs the best sales performance in HCM city and Medium cities (HN, Binh Duong, and Dong Nai), but in small cities, Hunh Tiu My is doing better.
