Zoop Health

- Coordinate with business and product team to create a minimal CRM for healthcare.
- Analyze clinics data, and provide dashboards to monitor their business performance.

Zoop product is designed for healthcare providers to manage their customer through interactions. The aim is to help them develop longer lasting relationships with their customers, drive business growth and increase customer loyalty.

Leading the business customer interviews and evaluating data quality

To make the connection between Clinics and their customers better, we have to quantify the value of quality and convenience of their interactions.

Quality of interactions

Is the feature going to help HP understand thoroughly their customers? For example,

  • Receive feedback promptly on product/service – quality, price, availability, others.
  • Monitor service experience - appointment response time, quality of communication, and other suggestions for improvement.
  • Feeling that HP cares their customers as a person via personalized content.
  • More one-on-one time with their customers via chat or reminder.

Convenience of interactions

Is the feature going to improve HP’s day-to-day workflow? For example,

  • Shorter wait times for appointments
  • Quicker access to appointments
  • Quicker update information related to services

Influencer Marketing Platform

- Buil a centralized logging system to track in-and-external users behaviors and activities.
- Design all-in-one dashboard for influencer profiles.

Hiip is a social media marketing platform which aims to connect brands and influencers, help them work together through social campaigns. We call it Influencer Marketing. Apart from Vietnam, we also served 2 other markets, Thailand and Indonesia at the same time.

Creating centralized Logging System to track user's behaviors

When I was carrying out some research into the user insight of real concern on campaigns, I found that both Brands and Influencers cared about the campaign, but Brands knew precisely their requirement, whereas Influencers hadn’t any idea at all. That means there were many conflicts between sharing information and working on content in every campaigns.

Therefore, we should track the routes from creating to reporting campaign to know which essential aspects influence user’s decisions. Then I, with the dev team, define all metrics (both user and business) to track user activities via actions and save params into the database.

Token System Engine Product

- Develop customer experience flow and monitor customer satisfaction.
- Build dashboard for customers to manage their business performance.

TSE product is a marketing service that is able to provide the token as another modern communication channel. These passes can only be used and applied as event tickets, cards, coupons and membership cards via a mobile device to make payment or scan information.

Mapping Experience Flow

Audi wants to find another communication channel (a direct communication but faster) that can replace the traditional phone calls. When a driver meets a big problem (e.g., car crash or car engine broke down), he calls Audi Call Center for support. Then the case manager must call to the third-party car repair and get the exact time to pick up the driver. After received a confirmation, she backs to the driver and proposed the time the car repair service will arrive. Then she has to wait for a response call from the driver or she takes the initiative to call the driver to know the problem is totally solved or not?

Then we propose the TSE for Audi client. The case manager can create cases (delivered via Apple passes), manage cases, search, or receive notifications). The driver can view the time and information of the car repair service provider, seed feedback of quality of Audi call center service.
